Our Wonderful plants

Let's try to consider the business of growing flowers from all angles and understand what is needed for the business and what profits can be expected.

Indoor flowers

Indoor flowers are grown in pots. If the plants are not exotic, then caring for them is not difficult. Even a beginner can cope with them. Most flowers can be grown in the home, but it is better to do business in a greenhouse or set aside a separate room in a country house.

Greenhouse flowers

Flowers in the greenhouse can also be grown outdoors, but in the northern regions they are grown in winter. This is done to receive flowers by February 14 and March 8. Caring for them is not easy, the competition is high, but if you organize the business correctly, you can make a lot of money.

Flowers for open ground

These can be all of the above flowers and are grown in season from spring to autumn. There is a lot of competition here, and work is done only during the season.

Medicinal plants and flowers

Medicinal plants are grown to obtain herbs to create teas, ointments and other things. You can sell it both dried and fresh. Virtually no care is required. The main thing is to get a healthy plant as a result of growing.

Seedlings of flowers

Flower seedlings are grown in a seasonal or year-round greenhouse from February to May. Sales are conducted until mid-June. The business is seasonal, but even from a small greenhouse you can get from 20 thousand plants, at 5-15 rubles apiece.

Saplings, bulbs

A separate view can be considered the idea of growing seedlings. For example, rose seedlings on the market cost from 200 to 400 rubles. It is not difficult to breed them, and from 1 square meter per year you can get several dozen high-quality seedlings. Bulbs are popular with tulips. They can be obtained in the open field.
